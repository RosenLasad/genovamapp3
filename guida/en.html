Quick guide

Genova mApp is an interactive map: explore, enable layers, open info cards, and view content.

In 30 seconds
  • Open Home and choose what to show (categories, routes, content).
  • Use zoom: more zoom = more details.
  • Tap a point: a card opens with photos, description, address, or content.

1) Navigation (basics)

  • Zoom: zoom in to see more points and details.
  • Drag to move around the map.
  • Tap a marker/point to open its card.
  • Close a card with the “X” or by tapping the map.

2) Home menu

The Home menu contains the main sections (categories, routes, content). Enable only what you need so the map stays clearer and faster.

3) Points and cards

  • Each point opens a card with information (title, description, address, images, or links).
  • Some categories may appear only at certain zoom levels, to avoid overcrowding.
  • If a point doesn’t seem “clickable”, zoom in 1–2 levels and try again.

4) Routes

Routes highlight a selection of “favorite” points connected together. They are designed to guide you simply, without forcing a rigid itinerary.

5) Language

You can change the language using the dedicated button. The guide loads automatically in the selected language (example: guida/it.html).

6) Events and content

When available, you may find panels with events, presentations, or editorial content (e.g., mini-documentaries). Tap the related button to open the list.

7) Practical tips

  • If you see too many elements: disable unnecessary categories or zoom out a bit.
  • On smartphones: portrait mode for exploring, landscape mode for more comfortable reading.
  • If the map feels “heavy”: close panels and keep only 1–2 categories active at a time.

8) Contacts

For reports, proposals, or collaborations, use the contacts provided in the project (website or official channels).
